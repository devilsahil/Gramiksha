Our Projects



PROJECT UDAAN

"Education is the golden key towards freedom." Project Udaan is a concept developed from the guidelines given by Madhya Pradesh Education Board. According to Right to Education Act 29, it has been mandatory to evaluate students based on co-scholastic areas and personal qualities. We believe in giving these young minds greater opportunities because their upliftment, in turn leads to a better and bright future for the nation itself and it also paves out new opportunities for these young buds to blossom. Project Udaan, which translates to uplift to greater heights focuses on promoting extra-curricular activities in school and to develop personal and social qualities in students on a basic level.
Our main focus on syllabus is on two things:

  • 1.Co-Scholastic Areas
  • 2.Personal & Social Qualities




JAGRUKTA

Jagrukta means alertness or awareness. Through this project, we want to bring into limelight a clearer picture of what these children go through and feel to a modern educated society which has turned a blind eye towards them. We believe in doing so through various art forms like poems, articles, paintings, photographs as art cab change and motivate many minds.



STREET MEET

We, at Gramiksha also aim to be Street Educators. It is our common belief that these children do not belong to the streets, they belong to one big family, just like each one of us. Getting a clearer picture towards their situation, their social boundaries and loving conditions, understanding them as young individuals, that is what we aim at. Then we identify the issues they face and try to provide the best of our service and support. Our community has so much to offer to see little souls who are waiting to be understood.



SWASTHYA

SWASTHYA, means health, which is considered to be the greatest wealth one can possess. With many diseases being eradicated and some still prevailing, we have a long way ahead. With certain advancements in technology and medical science, a child can be immunised and shielded against many such diseases which he is otherwise prone to. We aim to provide safe vaccines and immunisations which can be key factors to save a life. Being a part of the Child Welfare and Development Organization, our activities include providing supplementary nutrition, non-formal pre-school education, basic nutrition & health education, immunization, health check-ups and referral services. We also act as a bridge between these children and Anganwadi Centres who provide with essential medicines and Oral Rehydration Salts.

What's this all about?

Gramiksha is an NGO, formed by students. We aim to educate people in villages and promote education among the poverty-ridden and ignorant sector in India. We also provide them with books and conduct workshops to make education entertaining. Our work is to create interest among students for education. We donate books, install medical facilities, make the school’s environment hygienic and also organize activities and workshops. It is a recently formed organization and our team is composed of students and graduates from schools and universities. We don’t promise to make the entire India literate and change the circle of education, but we do promise to make a difference in Indian literacy rate and hopefully, it will do wonders for us. Its an “Initiative”, we finally decided to take !!!!
